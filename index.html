Wesley E. Joseph (he/him/they/them) is a bisexual 27-year-old author based in Belgium. They have a bachelor's degree in education, subjects English and computer science. Despite his degree, he chose to walk a different career path, and now works as a social media & community manager for a VR game developer.

When they're not writing, you can find them watching a TV-show or a film, submerging themself in a game, playing DnD, frothing over Doctor Who, or reading books.

Online, most people know him as Cobblewobbles. He streams on Twitch, where he shares his passion for storytelling through co-working / co-study streams. Although, occasionally, they also enjoy some downtime playing a (mostly story-driven) video game.

They also host a YouTube channel, mainly focused on video essays about games they adore, videos about their writing process, or just bookish content in general.
